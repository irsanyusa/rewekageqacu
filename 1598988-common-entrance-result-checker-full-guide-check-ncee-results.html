Publish date: 2024-06-18

Passing the National Common Entrance Examination (NCEE) is a significant achievement if you want to enrol in any Federal Unity College in Nigeria. After sitting the exams and the results are out, how do you check your scores using the Common Entrance Result checker? Knowing how to use the results checker is crucial for students, parents, and guardians. Here is a detailed guide on how to use the NCEE results checker.

National Common Entrance Examination (2024)

The National Common Entrance Examination is an annual national exam administered by Nigeria's National Examination Council (NECO). The exam is done by students who want to enrol in Junior Secondary School (JSS 1) at the Federal Unity Colleges in the country. The examinable subjects in the NCEE are:

How to use the Common Entrance Result checker (2024)

The Common Entrance result checker (2024) is an online platform where students who sat for the NCEE can access their results once the National Examination Council releases them. Even though the platform is easy to access and navigate, some people have experienced challenges that hinder them from enrolling in JSS1 on time. Here is a detailed guide on how to use the Common Entrance result checker.

  • Have the required information ready: There are a few prerequisites you must have to start the process of accessing your NCEE. The information you should have includes the examination number, serial number, and personal identification number (PIN). When you register for the exams, your school usually avails these essential details; if you forget them, you can get them for the learning institution.
  • Go to the NCEE official website: The official NCEE website is the only place to find reliable examination-related information, including your results. You need to log into the page or create an account if you don't have one.
  • Find the NCEE result section: Once logged into the website, navigate to the National Common Entrance Examination section and click the Check Results button.
  • Provide your examination information: On the page for checking results, you must provide details of your examination number, serial number, and PIN. Ensure you enter the details accurately, as the wrong information will be rejected.
  • Submit and view your results: When you are sure that you have entered the correct information, click the Submit button. Your results will be displayed on the screen shortly.
  • Save and print your results: Even though the objective is to view your results, you should have a hard copy for future reference and admission into a Federal Unity College. Therefore, saving and printing a copy of the results is advisable.

    Common problems when using the Common Entrance result checker

    Checking your NCEE results is easy, but occasionally, you might face a few hiccups. Below are common issues you will likely face on the platform and their possible remedy.

    • Forgotten personal information: You can easily forget all or one of your examination details, especially if you did not write them down somewhere. Since your PIN, serial number, and exam number are necessary, you should contact your school’s examination officer or NECO to help you retrieve them.
    • Website not loading: If you are having problems accessing the website, first ensure your internet connection is stable. Second, try accessing the website during off-peak hours when the website traffic is low.
    • Wrong details: You could be nervous when checking your results, and that may lead to entering incorrect information. When that happens, an error message will be displayed on the screen. Double-check the information you entered before submitting it again, and if the problem persists, contact your examination officer or NECO.

    Why should you check your NCEE results?

    There are several essential reasons for checking your NCEE results on time. Below are some of the reasons:

    • Results are a requirement for admission into Federal Unity Colleges. If you pass the exams highly, your chances of enrolling in a JSS of your choice are high, but a low pass limits your options.
    • You can start planning your next academic moves when you know your NCEE results. You can begin your early preparation for admission into JSS if you pass the exam, or you can look for alternative options if you fail.
    • Knowing your scores will remove the anxiety. Knowing your results will give you peace of mind, and you can start thinking of other important things.

    Advantages of using the Common Entrance result checker

    Using the NCEE result checker comes with multiple advantages. Here are a few benefits of using the online platform.

    When will the NCEE 2024 results be released?

    The National Examination Council (NECO) announces the release of the NCEE results. Typically, the results are released a few weeks after the examination date.

    Can I use my mobile phone to check my NCEE results?

    You can access the results using any internet-enabled device, including a smartphone. Open your phone’s browser and go to the NCEE website.

    What should I do when I cannot check my NCEE results?

    You may not access your results for various reasons, including poor internet and incorrect details. If you cannot access the results, contact your examination officer or NECO.
