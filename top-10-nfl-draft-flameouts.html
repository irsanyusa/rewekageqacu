Top 10 NFL Draft Flameouts

Publish date: 2024-08-16

“The Boz,” taken by the Seattle Seahawks with the first pick in the 1987 supplemental draft, had it all. Mohawk? Check. Bad acting chops in terrible movies (Stone Cold, One Tough Bastard)? Oh yeah. A signature on-field moment? Yes, sir: Monday Night Football, 1987, when Los Angeles Raiders running back Bo Jackson steamrolled over Bosworth on his way to the end zone. The only thing the University of Oklahoma linebacker was really missing? NFL talent.
