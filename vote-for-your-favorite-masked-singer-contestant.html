Our Prediction For ‘The Masked Singer’ Winner Is…

Turtle is our top pick at TR and is detective Kyle’s favorite to win. From the breathtaking stage presence to perfectly choreographed dances, this Masked Singer has the star quality everyone is cheering for. That is not to say you sleep on this character’s amazing voice.

Our second pick is Night Angel and she would be our first if only she wasn’t so inconsistent. When she’s good, she’s amazing but when she’s bad, she’s …

Our third pick is Frog, who is clearly a rapper, no points for guessing that. He’s got the swag, the stage presence and the style that makes him rank up on our prediction list.

Kitty And Rhino Will be In The Botton 2 On ‘Masked Singer’

Kitty was at risk after losing to the Frog in the top 7 but unfortunately doesn’t have the best vocals or the stage presence. Definitely can’t say the same about the costume, which is bomb.
