Pentatonix Perform a Stunning Rendition of Jennifer Hudson's Theme Song at her Talkshow

Publish date: 2024-09-01

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment

WireImage

Reading Time: 2 minutes

In a recent episode of The Jennifer Hudson Show Season 2, music enthusiasts were treated to a remarkable performance that combined the talents of Grammy Award-winning a cappella group Pentatonix and the show’s host, Jennifer Hudson. The main highlight of the episode was their captivating renditions of the Christian hymn “How Great Thou Art” and the show’s theme song, “Happy Place.”

Pentatonix Performs With Jennifer Hudson

Hudson joined forces with Pentatonix to perform a breathtaking rendition of “How Great Thou Art.” The performance showcases the remarkable harmonies and vocal abilities of Pentatonix, combined with the soulful and awe-inspiring voice of Hudson.

The energy between the artists was evident as they delivered a show-stopping rendition of their song. The seamless blend of their voices created a profound and moving interpretation of the timeless hymn.

As a surprise for the audience, Hudson and Pentatonix also performed the show’s theme song, “Happy Place” together. Their final duet brought an extra layer of warmth and cheer to the show.

Recently, Hudson discussed the backstory of her theme song. “Happy Place” is a tune known for its positive and energetic vibes, so it’s no surprise that she chose the song for her talk show theme. Interestingly enough, Hudson wrote this song 15 years ago, way before the show was around.

Pentatonix Talks About Their Busy Year, Being on The Masked Singer

While the musical performances were undeniably the stars of the show, Pentatonix also took the opportunity to share a glimpse into their eventful year. The group humorously recounted their busy schedule, joking about having only one night off to attend Scott Hoying‘s extravagant wedding.

This lighthearted banter provided a glimpse into the close-knit bond among the members of Pentatonix. To which some of whom have known each other since childhood.

Additionally, Pentatonix discussed their journey on The Masked Singer and how the show has propelled their careers to new heights. Their experiences on the show have certainly contributed to their growing success. Underlining the group’s versatility and adaptability in the music industry.

The harmonious collaboration between Hudson and Pentatonix, along with their light-hearted discussions about their year and The Masked Singer, made for a well-rounded and entertaining episode.
