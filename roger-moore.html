Roger Moore (Actor) - On This Day

Publish date: 2024-06-29

Profession: Actor

Biography: Roger Moore's most famous role was that of James Bond, and he is credited with bringing a lighter style to the character. In total Moore made 7 James Bond films, from "Live and Let Die"
in 1973 to "A View to a Kill" in 1985.

Moore rose to stardom playing Simon Templar in the British television show "The Saint", where he developed his suave persona. The show ran 1962-1969.

Moore became a goodwill ambassador for Unicef in 1991. He was knighted for his humanitarian work in 2003.

Born: October 14, 1927
Birthplace: London, United Kingdom

Generation: Greatest Generation
Chinese Zodiac: Rabbit
Star Sign: Libra

Died: May 23, 2017 (aged 89)
