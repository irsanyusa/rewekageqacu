Singer Marries Model, Shannon De Lima In Dominican Republic

Publish date: 2024-08-14

The 46-year-old New York-born celebrity of Pueto Rican roots married his Venezuelan model girlfriend, Shannon De Lima, 26, at his residence in the exclusive Dominican resort of Casa de Campo.

The couple have been dating off and on since 2012 after Anthony ended his marriage to pop singer, Jennifer Lopez, in 2011.

Anthony had dropped a hint on his Instagram account a few weeks ago that a "surprise" was in store after he posted a picture of himself and his lady love walking together.

Joining the couple's new life is De Lima's 7-year-old son, Daniel.

The salsa singer is already father to a set of twins, Max and Emme, 6, whom he shares with ex-wife, Lopez. He also has four kids from his previous relationships with Dayanara Torres and Debbie Rosado: Cristian, 13, Ryan, 11, Ariana, 20, and Chase.

Anthony is also an actor, appearing in TV and films. He starred with Lopez in the 2007 film "El Cantante," about the life of salsa pioneer Hector Lavoe.

Sources: Associated Press, Us Weekly.
