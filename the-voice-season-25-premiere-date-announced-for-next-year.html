The Voice is returning for Season 25 next year, and fans won’t have to wait long to see Dan + Shay join the show as the first coaching duo. NBC just announced that the new season will premiere in February 2024.

The Voice Season 25 Premiere Date Announced

The Voice is currently airing Season 24 of the show, which is heading into the Playoffs round this week. The season will come to a close in December, but fans will only have to wait a couple of months before Season 25 hits their TV screens.

NBC announced its midseason premiere dates this week. That includes Season 25 of The Voice, which kicks off with a two-part premiere on Monday, February 26, and Tuesday, February 27.

In case you missed it, Season 25 will feature longtime coach John Legend, as well as Reba McEntire in her second season on the show. Chance the Rapper, who coached in Season 23, will also be back. And the show will have its first coaching duo ever in country stars Dan + Shay.

Season 24 Is Heading into the Playoffs

NBC is currently airing Season 24 of The Voice, featuring Legend, McEntire, Niall Horan, and Gwen Stefani. The Knockouts round finished up last week, which means the show is heading into the Playoffs starting this Tuesday.

This season, the show introduced something called a “Super Save,” which allows each of the coaches to choose an eliminated artist from any team to come back and compete in the Playoffs. The artists who make it through this round will compete in the live shows.

The live finale of The Voice Season 24 is scheduled to air on NBC on Tuesday, December 19. Are you excited to watch the next season of the show? Let us know know in the comments below.
