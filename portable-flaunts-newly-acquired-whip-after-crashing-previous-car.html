Portable flaunts newly acquired whip after crashing previous car

Publish date: 2024-05-30

The artiste took to his Instagram with pictures of his new car, a BMW X3 on Wednesday, September 27, 2023. He posed shirtless next to the latest addition to his array of cars, flaunting the keys to the new car in his pictures.

He expressed gratitude to God in his caption saying, "Zazuu Am Nothing Without God. O De Le Lo Far Without God." His new post was received with congratulatory messages and comments from most, while others taunted the singer for acquiring an older model of the car.

This comes after Portable was involved in a dreadful accident on Wednesday, July 12, 2023, in his luxurious vehicle. According to the singer, the incident took place at Osapa London, Lekki while he was driving to his music studio during a heavy downpour.

He showed the damage to his car on his Instagram and went live shortly after to assure his fans of his safety promising his fans that he was unharmed. He also expressed his confidence in his musical abilities, vowing to buy a new vehicle soon.

The controversial artist said, "Hustle wey buy me that one go buy another one. You can’t ruin my life; I buy moto, dem use mouth spoil am. The car didn’t kill me."

Portable also swore to buy himself a house and a private jet soon.
