List of Catalan People

  • 1608-11-23 Francisco Manuel de Mello, Portuguese writer (Catalan Revolt), born in Lisbon, Portugal (d. 1666)
  • 1618-09-09 Joan Cererols, Catalan musician, composer and monk, born in Martorell (d. 1680)
  • 1729-12-03 Antonio Soler, Spanish Catalan composer (Fandango), born in Olot, Catalonia, Spain (d. 1783)
  • 1801-01-21 Ramon Vilanova y Barrera, Catalan composer and choirmaster, born in Barcelona, Spain (d. 1870)
  • 1809-11-26 Marià Obiols, Catalan composer, born in Barcelona (d. 1888)
  • 1814-05-30 Eugène Charles Catalan, French-Belgian mathematician (worked on continued fractions, descriptive geometry, number theory and combinatorics), born in Bruges, Belgium (d. 1894)
  • 1835-04-06 José Marraco y Ferrer, Catalan church musician and composer, born in Barcelona, Spain (d. 1913)
  • 1852-06-25 Antoni Gaudí, Catalan-Spanish architect (Sagrada Família, Casa Milà, Casa Batlló), born in Reus, Catalonia, Spain (d. 1926)
  • 1854-06-19 Alfredo Catalani, Italian operatic composer, born in Lucca, Grand Duchy of Tuscany (d. 1893)
  • 1872-07-07 Juan Lamote de Grignon, Catalan pianist, composer (La nit de Nadal (Christmas Night); Hespèria), and orchestra leader (Cobla Barcelona; València Municipal Orchestra), born in Barcelona, Spain (d. 1949)
  • 1875-03-12 Juli Garreta, Spanish-Catalan composer (Sardanes; Les illes Medes), born in Sant Feliu de Guíxols, Spain (d. 1925)

  • 1880-10-07 Jaume Pahissa, Spanish-Catalan composer (Gala Placidia; Aurora de esperanza), and musicologist, born in Barcelona, Spain (d. 1969)
  • 1882-09-28 Eugenio d'Ors, Spanish Catalan essayist and philosopher, born in Barcelona, Spain (d. 1954)
  • 1888-06-18 Margarita Xirgu, Catalan stage actress, director and political radical (Bloody Wedding, Mariana Pineda), born in Barcelona, Spain (d. 1969)
  • 1892-02-04 Andreu Nin, Catalan politician (d. 1937)
  • 1893-04-16 Federico Mompou, Catalan composer (Música Callada -'The Voice of Silence'), born in Barcelona, Spain (d. 1987)

  • 1896-09-25 Roberto Gerhard, Spanish Catalan composer (the Plague), born in Valls, Catalonia, Spain (d. 1970)
  • 1897-08-12 Manuel Blancafort, Spanish-Catalan pianist and composer, born in La Garriga, Spain (d. 1987)
  • 1906-06-21 Lluís Maria Millet, Catalan composer, born in Barcelona, Spain (d. 1990)
  • 1908-10-10 Mercè Rodoreda, Catalan novelist (La plaça del diamant), born in Barcelona (d. 1983)
  • 1923-11-01 Victoria de los Ángeles, Catalan operatic lyric soprano, born in Barcelona, Spain (d. 2005)
  • 1924-01-14 Guy Williams [Armando Joseph Catalano], American actor (Zorro; Lost in Space), born in New York City (d. 1989)
  • 1931-02-09 Xavier Benguerel, Catalan composer (Llibre Vermell; Jo, Dalí), born in Barcelona, Spain (d. 2017)
  • 1931-11-27 Juan Guinjoan, Catalan composer and pianist, born in Riudoms, Spain (d. 2019)
  • 1933-09-22 Leonardo Balada, Catalan American composer, born in Barcelona, Spain
  • 1935-10-18 Jorge "Jordi" Cervelló, Catalan composer and musicologist, born in Barcelona, Spain
  • 1942-03-15 Montserrat Figueras, Catalan soprano and early music advocate (Hespèrion), born in Barcelona, Spain (d. 2011)
  • 1947-01-13 Carles Rexach, former Spanish-Catalan footballer and coach
  • 1959-09-30 Ettore Messina, Italian basketball coach (Olimpia Milano), born in Catalania, Italy
  • 1962-12-29 Carles Puigdemont [i Casamajó], Catalan politician and nationalist (President of Catalonia, 2016-17), born in Amer, Catalonia, Spain
