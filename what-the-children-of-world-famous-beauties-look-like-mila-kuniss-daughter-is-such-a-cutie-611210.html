What the Children ofWorld-Famous Beauties Look Like (Mila Kuniss Daughter IsSuch aCutie)

Publish date: 2024-07-26

It happens that even the most good-looking couples have pretty ordinary kids. But sometimes it happens otherwise, a child inherits the best features of their parents and the only word that can describe them is “charming.” In this article, we would like to show you how the children of the most beautiful ladies of Hollywood look.

We at Bright Side found out that almost all the famous moms from our article have 2 and sometimes even 3 children.

Jennifer Lopez

Jessica Alba

Jessica is a mother of 3. Her first daughter Honor was born in 2008. Her second daughter Haven was born in 2011.

Her third child is a son named Hayes. He was born in 2017.

Natalie Portman

Portman has 2 children. Her elder son Aleph was born in 2011.

This is her daughter Amalia (2017).

Megan Fox

Megan is the mother of 3 sons. The eldest Noah turns 6 this year.

Her second child Bodhi was born in 2014.

And her third son Journey was born in 2016.

Mila Kunis

Her son Dimitri was born in 2016. It seems that by choosing this name Mila decided to pay tribute to her Ukrainian roots.

Halle Berry

Halle has a son and a daughter from different marriages. Her daughter Nahla was born in 2008.

Her son Maceo was born in 2013.

Milla Jovovich

Milla is the mother of 2 daughters. Her elder one Ever was born in 2007.

Her younger daughter Dashiel was born in 2015.

Gwyneth Paltrow

Her son Moses is 2 years younger. He was born in 2006.

Denise Richards

Denise Richards has 2 biological daughters and one adopted daughter. Her elder daughter’s name is Sam (2004).

Her second daughter is Lola, and she’s just a year younger than her sister.

Denise adopted her third daughter Eloise in 2011, right after her birth.

Gwen Stefani

The singer and the actress Gwen Stefani has 3 sons. Her elder son Kingston was born in 2006.

Her second son Zuma was born in 2008.

And the youngest Apollo was born in 2014.
