Aunjanue Ellis’s early life

Aunjanue L. Ellis was born on 21st February 1969 in San Francisco, California, USA. She was raised by her grandmother alongside her mother at McComb, Mississippi farm.

The identity of her parents remains a mystery. Also, she has a sister whose name is Sasha Ellis.

Her passion to act started from childhood. Often, she performed recitations and skits at her church, New Home Baptist Church. These activities somehow significantly helped her to nature her acting skills.
