Arnold H. Glasow (born as Arnold Henry Glasow in Fond du Lac, Wisconsin, U.S., died in Freeport, Illinois) was a famous Businessman from USA, who lived between 1905 and 1998.

Arnold Henry Glasow graduated from Ripon College, and started his own business when he moved to Freeport just after the depression.

His business was a humor magazine that he marketed to firms nationally, which firms would turn it into their "house publications" to send to their customers. He carried on this business for over 60 years, publishing his first book at age 92.
