David Archuleta has recently shared a sneak peek at his upcoming role of Joseph in Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat. He begins in this role on July 16 in Ivins, Utah.

On Friday July 8, Archuleta shared a fun video of him in the iconic technicolor dreamcoat. Within the video he twirls around with a smile on his face. Archuleta is now only days away until the beginning of his role at The Tuacahn Amphitheatre. According to the theatre’s website, he will star as Joseph from July 16 to September 3.

For those who may not know, Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat is a story based on the character of Joseph from the Bible’s Book of Genesis. The show features music, costumes, laughter, joy, and heart. This show features multiple different types of music, including pop/rock to country, musical comedy, and more. Fans of Archuleta’s are excited to see him return to singing after a lengthy vocal rest.

Ahead of this video of the singer, he shared an Instagram reel where he switched up his hair. Archuleta recently went back to his natural hair color, after having a blonde moment for a few months.

David Archuleta Comes Off of Vocal Rest

Archuleta’s 2022 began a bit rocky when he was forced to cancel his “OK, All Right” tour. Even though the singer was excited to finally begin this tour after years of rescheduling, he fell ill. Before his first concert, he announced via Instagram that his doctor ordered vocal rest for him. It was later revealed that he was diagnosed with a hemorrhaged vocal cord. As a result, the singer underwent vocal cord surgery in March.

Throughout the healing journey, Archuleta stayed on vocal rest per doctor request. In May, he participated in the American Idol reunion episode alongside Jordin Sparks, David Cook, Kris Allen and more. Near the end of May, Archuleta was taken off of vocal rest and sharing singing content to social media. Fans are happy to hear Archuleta singing once again.
